# Metro Cafe Ordering Guide and Customer Service Manual

Welcome to Metro Cafe! This manual details our operational menu and order customization policies.

## 1. Beverage Offerings

### Espresso Drinks
- **Espresso**: Single shot ($2.50), Double shot ($3.50).
- **Americano**: Hot or iced. Standard water and espresso.
- **Caffe Latte**: Espresso with steamed milk and a thin layer of foam.
- **Cappuccino**: Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

### Customization Options
- **Sizes**: Tall (12 oz), Grande (16 oz), Venti (20 oz).
- **Milk Selections**: Whole milk (default), Skim milk (no charge). Premium alternatives: Oat milk (extra $0.60), Almond milk (extra $0.60).
- **Temperature**: Extra hot, standard hot, or iced.

## 2. Food & Pastry Catalog

### Baked Goods
- **Bagels**: Plain, Sesame, or Everything. Served with butter or cream cheese.
- **Muffins**: Freshly baked Blueberry or Double Chocolate.
- **Croissants**: Butter croissants and Almond croissants.
