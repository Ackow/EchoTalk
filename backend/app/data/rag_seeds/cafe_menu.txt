## 2. Order Customization & Surcharges [user] [chinese:定制选项及服务费]

During rush hour, baristas must ask and customize every order according to customer preferences:

### 📏 Sizes
- **Tall**: 12 oz (approx. 350ml) - *Default 1 espresso shot*
- **Grande**: 16 oz (approx. 470ml) - *Default 2 espresso shots*
- **Venti**: 20 oz (approx. 590ml) - *Default 2 espresso shots (3 shots for iced versions)*

### 🥛 Milk Options
- **Standard (No Charge)**:
  - **Whole Milk**: Default milk for lattes and cappuccinos, offering the richest flavor.
  - **Skim Milk / Non-fat Milk**: Lighter option with no fat.
- **Alternatives (Surcharge $0.60)**:
  - **Oat Milk**: Very popular! Creamy texture with a natural oat flavor, perfect for lattes.
  - **Almond Milk**: Nutty flavor and lower calories.
  - **Soy Milk**: High plant-protein with a slightly sweet taste.

### 🍯 Sweeteners & Syrups (Surcharge $0.50)
- **Syrup Flavors**: Vanilla, Caramel, Hazelnut, Sugar-Free Vanilla.
- **Sweetener Packets**: Sugar in the Raw, Splenda / Stevia.
- **Sweetness Control**: **"Half-sweet"** or **"Half-pump"** (reduces syrup pumps by half).

### 🌡️ Temperature & Ice Levels
- **Hot Options**: Standard Hot (~150°F / 65°C) or **Extra Hot** (~170°F / 77°C - recommended for takeaway).
- **Iced Options**: Standard Ice, **Light Ice** (less ice, more beverage), or **No Ice**.

## 4. Barista Standard Service Workflow [ai] [chinese:咖啡师标准服务流程]

To keep the queue moving quickly in a busy Manhattan cafe, baristas must follow these steps:

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[1. Greeting & Name Entry] --> B[2. Core Order & Size Selection]
    B --> C[3. Milk & Temperature Customizations]
    C --> D[4. Upsell Suggestion: Food/Pastries]
    D --> E[5. Confirm Order & Dine-in/To-go]
    E --> F[6. Payment Processing & Name on Cup]
```

1. **Friendly Greeting**: *"Hi there! Welcome to Metro Cafe. What can I get started for you?"*
2. **Size & Customization Inquiry**: If they order a latte/cappuccino, immediately ask: *"What size would that be?"* and *"Any milk preferences like oat or soy?"*
3. **Upsell Food**: *"Would you like to pair that with a warm blueberry muffin or avocado toast today?"*
4. **Dining Preference**: *"Is that for here or to go?"* (Or *"Dine-in or takeaway?"*)
5. **Payment & Name Collection**: *"Perfect! That comes to $X.XX. How would you like to pay today? And could I get a name for your cup?"*

## 5. Essential Cafe Vocabulary & Oral Practice Notes [user] [chinese:基础词汇与口语表达]

### 💁 Barista Common Phrases (How to Ask)
* *"What can I get started for you today?"* (Greeting customers at the counter)
* *"What size can I get for you? We have Tall, Grande, and Venti."* (Inquiring about cup sizes)
* *"Would you like room for cream?"* (Asked for drip/black coffee or Americano to leave space for milk)
* *"Dine-in or to-go?"* / *"For here or to go?"* (Asking for dining preference)
* *"Do you want that warmed up?"* (For warming up muffins, bagels, or bread)

### 🙋 Customer Common Phrases (How to Order)
* *"Can I get a Grande iced oat milk latte, please?"* (Ordering a customized iced latte)
* *"I'd like a double-shot espresso, macchiato style."* (Ordering espresso "marked" with a dollop of foam)
* *"Make it half-decaf and extra hot."* (Specifying temperature and decaf preference)
* *"Hold the whipped cream, please."* (Requesting no whipped cream)
* *"Can I have that toasted with cream cheese on the side?"* (Ordering a toasted bagel with cream cheese served separately)

### ☕ Specialty Coffee Terminology (Slang)
* **Decaf**: Decaffeinated coffee.
* **Half-Caff**: A blend of half regular espresso shots and half decaf shots.
* **Dirty Chai**: A Chai Latte with an added shot of espresso.
* **Espresso Macchiato**: Espresso "marked" with a dollop of warm milk foam.
* **Single Origin**: Coffee beans sourced from a single farm or region.
